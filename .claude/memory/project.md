## alwayshere.co.il

- **Type**: E-commerce website — personal gifts niche
- **Platform**: WordPress + WooCommerce
- **Status**: Early setup / greenfield (as of 2026-03-21)
- **Namespace prefix**: `alwayshere_` (functions, hooks, options, meta keys)

### Business Goals
- Sell personalized/custom gifts
- Strong SEO strategy (product pages, category pages, blog)
- Social media campaigns — must be visually appealing and shareable
- Modern, engaging UX — conversion-focused design
- Target audience: gift buyers (birthdays, anniversaries, holidays)

### Design Direction
- Modern, appealing, user-engaging
- Optimized for social sharing (OG tags, rich snippets, product images)
- Mobile-first (social traffic is predominantly mobile)
